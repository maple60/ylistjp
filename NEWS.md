# jpplantnames 0.1.0

- Initial release.
- Added cached access to the Vascular Plant Japanese Name Checklist ver. 1.10.
- Added helpers for looking up scientific names from Japanese plant names:
  `scientific_name()`, `japanese_name_search()`, `japanese_name_suggest()`,
  and `japanese_name_info()`.
- Added optional WFO Plant List and GBIF species match helpers:
  `wfo_suggest()`, `wfo_accepted_name()`, and `gbif_match()`.
- Added English and Japanese pkgdown documentation.
